Clockwatcher

Clockwatcher is a test application that generates a nōtif with the current time once a minute. When you initially sign up for nōtifs from Clockwatcher (or when Clockwatcher restarts), it generates and sends a new notif. Thereafter, it updates this nōtif once per minute until midnight, and then creates a new one. Nōtifs from Clockwatcher have an expiration 24 hours in the future.

To sign up for nōtifs from Clockwatcher, fill in the address of your nōtif agent and press the "Nōtify me!" button below. Your nōtif agent will prompt you to confirm that you want to receive notifs from Clockwatcher; you can edit the name and maximum priority Clockwatcher is allowed to send. The name is only used to help you keep track of what nōtifs you have authorized. Clockwatcher sends its nōtifs at the lowest priority, Informational, so the priority setting doesn't matter.
